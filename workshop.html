About the Workshop

Since 2023, I have co-organized the annual Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop. Started in 2018, this initiative is a joint effort of Prof. David Keiser (UMass Amherst), Prof. Cathy Kling (Cornell), and Prof. Dan Phaneuf (University of Wisconsin-Madison), in collaboration with the Cornell Atkinson Center for Sustainability and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). The workshop serves as a platform for economists, policy experts, engineers, and other professionals to discuss Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) for water quality.

Objectives

The event is designed to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and policy dialogue on IAMs for water quality.
